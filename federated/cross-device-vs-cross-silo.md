The primary FL axis. **Cross-device**: large fleets of weak/intermittent devices (phones), optimising for scale, bandwidth and participation. **Cross-silo**: a small number of institutions (hospitals, councils), optimising for trust, governance, legal separation and coordination. UK public-sector federation is overwhelmingly cross-silo.
