# Marriage Allowance

Marriage Allowance lets the lower earner of a couple transfer some of their Personal Allowance to their husband, wife or civil partner. If they are eligible this will then lower their tax bill. The sandbox endpoint is `https://test-api.service.hmrc.gov.uk`.
