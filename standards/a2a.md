Launched by [Google](../organisations/google.md) in April 2025 and donated to the [Linux Foundation](../organisations/linux-foundation.md) mid-2025. Uses JSON [Agent Cards](../glossary/agent-card.md) for capability discovery, with long-running tasks, streaming and enterprise auth. A2A states explicitly that it and MCP address different problems.
