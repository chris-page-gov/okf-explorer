# Guidance

Structured legislation can be obtained as CLML (`data.xml`), Akoma Ntoso (`data.akn`), XHTML (`data.xht`/`data.htm`), HTML5 (`data.html`), PDF (`data.pdf`), CSV and RDF/XML where advertised. Lists and searches use Atom (`data.feed`).
