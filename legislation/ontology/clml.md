# Model

CLML 2.6 supports primary, secondary and EU-origin legislation. The Explorer normalizes `Group`, `Part`, `Chapter`, `Pblock`, `PsubBlock`, `P1group`–`P3group`, `P1`–`P7`, `Schedule`, `Appendix`, EU divisions and attachments while preserving source element names, IDs, status, extent and passage text.
